# History

## v1
Initial public release of the figure-regeneration code and redistributable derived data for the arXiv
paper "Kernel Density Estimation by Spectral Decomposition: Data-Driven Tapering and Superposition."
Includes the figure scripts, the derived CRSP results, the vendored random-beacon sample, and a
one-command `make_figures.sh`. Mixture results use a self-contained BIC Gaussian-mixture EM with no
external mixture dependency.
